Grill Mastery Made Simple
  • Ultimate 8-in-1
    Z Grills can expertly grill, smoke, bake, roast, sear, braise, BBQ, and char-grill. Get superior, wood-fired flavor without the messiness and time constraints of charcoal or gas grills.
  • How it works
    The auger at the bottom of the hopper pulls the hardwood pellets into the fire pot where they contact the hot rod and ignite the pellet grill.
  • Set it and forget it
    Its digital temperature control and consistent, convection-based heat works like an oven, allowing you to walk away and enjoy the company of friends and family.
  • Savory, wood-smoked flavors
    Use a variety of wood pellet flavors such as pecan, oak, hickory and mesquite that add big, bold flavors to your food.

About the Manufacturer
Z Grills is a U.S-based brand that has been making high-quality grills and smokers for more than 30 years. With warehouses in Los Angeles, California and Atlanta, Georgia, they have exported top-of-the-line products to Europe and all around the United States.
